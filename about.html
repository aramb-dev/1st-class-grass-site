About Us

Our Story

First Class Grass was founded by Abdur-Rahman Bilal with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable lawn care services to the Harlowe Point and Pagehurst neighborhoods in Durham, NC.

As a local resident, Abdur-Rahman noticed that many of his neighbors struggled to find consistent, quality lawn care at reasonable prices. Many services were either too expensive or unreliable, leaving homeowners frustrated.

That's when he decided to start First Class Grass - a service dedicated exclusively to these neighborhoods, offering simple, transparent pricing and consistent bi-weekly service that homeowners could count on.

Our Approach

At First Class Grass, we believe in keeping things simple. We focus on doing a few things exceptionally well rather than offering a wide range of services that compromise on quality.

Our subscription-based model ensures that your lawn receives regular, consistent care without the hassle of scheduling appointments or negotiating prices each time.

We're committed to:

  • Reliability - we show up when scheduled, every time
  • Quality - professional equipment and techniques
  • Affordability - transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Community focus - serving only our local neighborhoods
