About Us

Our Story

1st Class Grass (also known as 1st Class Grass) was rebranded from Aram Lawn Services in 2018. Our founder has been mowing lawns for over 5 years, bringing extensive experience to every property we service.

After moving to a new house where the HOA doesn't cover lawn care, we recognized an opportunity to help our neighbors with professional lawn maintenance. What started as a service for our immediate block has grown into a vision to serve our entire complex.

Our team includes experienced professionals who have been in the lawn care industry for 5-7 years. We've combined our expertise to create a simple, reliable subscription service that provides bi-weekly lawn care at affordable rates for the Harlowe Point and Pagehurst neighborhoods in Durham, NC.

Our Approach

At 1st Class Grass, we believe in keeping things simple. We focus on doing a few things exceptionally well rather than offering a wide range of services that compromise on quality.

Our subscription-based model ensures that your lawn receives regular, consistent care without the hassle of scheduling appointments or negotiating prices each time.

We're committed to:

  • Reliability - we show up when scheduled, every time
  • Quality - professional equipment and techniques
  • Affordability - transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Community focus - serving only our local neighborhoods
